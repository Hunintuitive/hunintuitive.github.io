Affordable IT Services for Individuals

Nov 9, 2024 · 4 min read

🌐 Explore the digital world with my help!

I offer you a range of computing services designed to make your life easier. I am happy to offer my help and expertise to anyone who has limited resources, cannot afford very expensive “professional” services, but needs some form of one-off or even regular help at an affordable price. It is in this area that I offer my services as a private individual, in a field where most service providers do not offer help.

My Background: With over 8 years of experience in IT customer and desktop support, I have honed my skills as a Remote Desktop Manager Specialist. Throughout my career, I have successfully assisted a diverse range of clients, addressing a variety of IT challenges with precision and dedication.

I have provided comprehensive support to both business clients and personal acquaintances in the following areas:

  • Desktop Support: Proficient in Windows XP, 7, 10, 11, and Linux desktop operating systems.
  • Software Support: Expertise in Microsoft Office, software installation and removal, and basic scripting/CLI usage.
  • Remote Access Solutions: Experienced with Citrix and remote OS installation utilizing SCCM, as well as supporting virtual desktop infrastructure (VDI), or individual support via TeamViewer.
  • Network Support: Skilled in managing VPN connectivity and resolving network-related issues.
  • User Management: Proficient in Active Directory user and computer management, handling user profiles.
  • Hardware Support: Extensive experience in supporting laptops, PCs, and mobile devices, including printer and thin client support.

My approach combines technical expertise with a commitment to customer service, allowing me to effectively resolve issues while fostering positive relationships with my clients. I am passionate about leveraging technology to enhance user experiences.

My services include:

  • Training & Education:
    • 🖥️Computer Education -Unlock the potential of technology with our comprehensive computer education program. We offer tailored courses ranging from basic digital literacy to advanced software applications. Outcome: By the end of the program, you will confidently navigate various software, significantly improving your productivity and enhancing your success opportunities.

  • Technical Support:
    • 🔧Hardware and Software Help -Experiencing issues with your computer? I offer you assitance for both hardware and software problems. From troubleshooting to resolving complex issues, I’ve got you covered.
